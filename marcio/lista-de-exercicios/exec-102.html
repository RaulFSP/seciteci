What exactly is freeCodeCamp? freeCodeCamp is a community of people from all around the world who are learning to code together. We're a 501(c)(3) public charity.

How will freeCodeCamp help me learn to code? You will learn to code by building dozens of projects, step-by-step, right in your browser, code editor, or mobile app. You will also earn free verified certifications along the way.

Is freeCodeCamp really free? Yes. Every aspect of freeCodeCamp is 100% free. The courses, the projects, and even the certifications.

>Can freeCodeCamp help me get a job as a software developer? Yes. Every year, thousands of people who join the freeCodeCamp community get their first software developer job.
